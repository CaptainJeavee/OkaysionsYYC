About us

Remembered always
Life is an event. Make it memorable.




Okaysions YYC
-We are a wedding/event planning company in the Canada that offers end-to-end (FULL) planning service to our clientele from conceptualization, vendor recommendation, time and budget management to on the day coordination. As we have done numerous local and destination events as well as big bridal fairs, event planning is indeed our cup of tea. From dreaming up a theme and color palette to recommending suppliers, booking them, meeting and liaising with them down to the most diminutive details, we take care of these steps while assuring every client that they will have a stress-free preparation and a refined celebration.


Mission

The Okaysions YYC. our mission is not merely to survive in the ever-evolving event industry but to thrive with so much passion, compassion, class, and style. May it be a large-scale celebration or an intimate occasion, the company focuses on the intricate details of the whole event ensuring that each element and idea will be executed with the highest quality while carrying our company insignia.

Okaysions YYC provides to best services possible ensuring your event will be executed flawlessly, along with our team we will ensure that the services you will get in your events will be perfectly executed.
